HOME LOST OR FOUND HOW IT WORKS ABOUT US

This website offers a simple, confidential and secure way of getting back your lost items from anywhere in India.

1. All you have to do is select report type, whether it is lost item or found item.

2. Select the article type that you have lost or find (eg: document, wallet).

3. Provide lost/found date and time

4. Enter the place where the item is lost/found

5. Enter the nearest police station

6. Enter the article description



The information submitted will be confidential, it will reach to superior(police) they will collect the item or return the item based on your report type.
